shape from shading

Recover a 3D surface from shading information. Move the light source to different directions and capture at least three photos — the normal map and elevation model reconstruct automatically via photometric stereo and Frankot–Chellappa integration.

  1. Drag the azimuth and elevation sliders to choose a lighting direction.
  2. Release to capture a photo at that angle. Repeat with 3 distinct directions.
  3. The normal map, gradient integral, and reconstructed DEM update after each capture.
  4. Press reset to discard all photos and start over.
